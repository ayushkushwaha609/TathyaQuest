Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Tathya ("the App") is an AI-powered fact-checking application built by Ayush Kushwaha. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Data you provide

  • Video URLs you submit for fact-checking

Data we generate automatically

  • Device ID — a randomly generated anonymous identifier stored on your device, used to track daily usage limits
  • Usage logs — timestamps and URLs of fact-checks you perform, linked to your device ID and (if signed in) your account
  • Google account info (if you sign in) — your email address and display name, retrieved via Google Sign-In

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment information
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or local files
  • We do not track your location

How We Use Your Data

Data Purpose
Device ID Enforce daily free usage limits
Usage logs Track per-platform check counts; improve the service
Google account Unlock higher daily limits; identify your session
Video URLs Send to AI services for transcription and fact-checking

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Service Purpose
Google Sign-In Optional authentication
Groq API Audio transcription & LLM fact-checking
Google Gemini YouTube video analysis
Sarvam AI Text-to-speech in Indian languages
RapidAPI Audio extraction from videos
MongoDB Atlas Secure cloud database

Video URLs you submit are sent to these services solely to provide the fact-checking functionality.

Data Retention

  • Cached fact-checks are stored in our database indefinitely to serve faster repeat results
  • Usage logs are retained for up to 90 days
  • Google account info is retained until you sign out of the App
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Contact below)

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
